introduce

Liverpool is one of the most successful clubs in England, they have won the English league 19 times, won 7 FA Cups, 8 League Cups, 15 Super Cups, 1 Football League Super Cup, 6 UEFA Champions League , 3 UEFA Europa Leagues, 4 European Super Cups and 1 FIFA Club World Cup.

Liverpool was founded on 3 June 1892 by John Houlding and ascended to the national league the following year. The team has used Anfield's home ground since its inception until now. The team was very successful in the 70s and 80s, under coaches Bill Shankly and Bob Paisley, they won 11 national championships and 7 European titles.

Liverpool has a strong and very loyal fan base. Their longtime rivals are the neighboring team Everton and the team Manchester United. The team's traditional song is "You'll Never Walk Alone". Liverpool players first wore all red in 1964, when coach Bill Shankly thought that red would have an impact on the opponent's mentality.

For much of Liverpool's history its home colours have been all red, but when the club was founded its kit was more like the contemporary Everton kit. The blue and white quartered shirts were used until 1894, when the club adopted the city's colour of red.[3] The city's symbol of the liver bird was adopted as the club's badge in 1901.
